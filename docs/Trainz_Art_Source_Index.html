Content Creation Art Source DVD

This art source DVD contains all artwork created by the Auran Trainz 'Brew Crew' over the past three years up to the release of Trainz Railroad Simulator 2004 SP2.

These disks contain the source files for both Trainz released content as well as previously un-released content. Please note however that it is Auran created content only, and does not include any third party content.

By using this content, you are agreeing to the following Licensing Agreement. Please read this carefully before you continue.

You are free to edit, upgrade, or change any of the content included so long as all updates are distributed through Auran's Trainz Download Station and that no part of the content is converted into any other game format as per the licensing agreement.

We have added 3D Studio Max files, Gmax files where available, Photoshop files, TGA and BMP files, and WAV files where applicable. There is so much content that it has filled the equivalent of 10 CDs. We have compressed these to fit on two CDs - now combined onto one DVD.

All included content is available only as a starting point for your own editing and production. Therefore the level of texture and model completion (and quality) may vary.

You may release any included content under your own KUID. You don't even need to credit us, although that would be nice!

Auran hopes you find this art source useful, and we hope to see your new creations on the Download Station very soon.

Installation

To install the 10 content packs you will require a decompression program such as WinZip. You may download WinZip here.

Please note that if you decompress the content packs, you will require over 6 Gigabytes of free space on your hard drive.

To access the content packs, simply click on the relevant pack number below. You will be prompted to save or open the Zip file.
